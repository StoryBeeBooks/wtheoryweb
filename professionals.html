Core Web Infrastructure & Hosting

AI-First Cloud Architecture

We host your site using static, serverless technology on the Global Content Delivery Network.

Why this matters:

Unlike slow WordPress servers that block bots, our static architecture allows AI Crawlers (like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity) to index your business data instantly.

The Benefit:

This "Machine-Readable" speed directly boosts your GEO (Geo-targeted Engine Optimization) score, ensuring your business details are accurately fed into the AI systems that customers use to find local services.

Managed Domain Registration

Search, registration, and annual renewal of one .com or .ca domain name.

DNS Management

Professional configuration of A Records, CNAMEs, and TXT records to ensure your domain is verified and healthy.

SSL Security Certificate

Implementation of https:// encryption (Let's Encrypt) to ensure the "Secure" padlock appears in browsers, which is a mandatory ranking signal for Google.

99.9% Uptime Guarantee

Your site lives on a distributed network, meaning it does not go down even if one server fails.

Zero-Database Security

The site is built with pure HTML5/CSS/JS. Because there is no database to hack and no plugins to update, your site is virtually immune to the malware attacks that plague standard small business websites.

Custom Design & Architecture

A dedicated virtual consultation to understand your business, discuss your brand vision, review content requirements, and align on design preferences before we begin building your site.

5-Page Architecture

  • Home Page: Hero section with "Call to Action" buttons, Trust Badges, and Review Snippets.
  • About Us: Personal bio section, "Our Story," and Team photos.
  • Services Menu: A detailed breakdown of offerings (e.g., "Pre-Purchase Inspection" vs "Tarion Warranty Inspection").
  • FAQ & Knowledge Share: Accordion-style FAQ section for common customer questions.
  • Get a Quote / Contact: Optimized intake form or direct "Click-to-Call" functionality.

Instant Lead Notification System Web3Forms

We implement secure, serverless contact forms using Web3Forms technology.

How it works:

When a customer fills out the "Request a Quote" form, the details are routed directly to your preferred email inbox (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, etc.) in real-time.

The Benefit:

You never miss a lead. There is no complicated "dashboard" to log into to check messages; if your phone pings with an email, you have the lead.

Sticky Header

Navigation bar that stays visible while scrolling (includes Phone Number).

Accessibility Compliance
